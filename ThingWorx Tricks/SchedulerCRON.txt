1. CRON format:
A B C D E F G
A - Second(0-59)
B - Minute(0-59)
C - Hour(0-23)
D - Day(1-31)
E - Month(1-12)
F - Day of Week(xxx, MON/TUE/WED/THU/FRI/SAT/SUN)
G - Year(xxxx)

2. Symbols
? - unspecified
* - everything
- - range
, - multiple value delimiter
/ - every x values

3.Example:
0 0 3? * SAT *
Means: at 3:00:00 each Saturday, every month, every year
